Lampung Lampung's

Local Food

Muli Chips

Muli Banana is a typical plant from Lampung. Bananas in Lampung are usually processed into chips.

Engkak

Engkak is a typical Lampung sticky rice cake, it is like the Lapis Legit but it is softer and lasts longer.

Gagodoh

A typical Lampung snack made from bananas with the addition of wheat flour.

Seruit

Fried or grilled fish dishes mixed with chili shrimp paste, tempoyak (fermented durian), and mango.

Segubal

Food made from sticky rice and coconut milk wrapped in banana leaves and how to cook it by steaming.

Pisro

Fish cooked with a variety of delicious spices, red chili so it feels spicy, the addition of sweet palm sugar, and thick gravy

Gulai Taboh

Fish added with bamboo shoots, vegetable leaves of Tangkil, and long beans.

Serbat Kweni

A typical Lampung drink made from sugar water, kweni mango, and basil seeds.

Durian Luwak Coffee

The drink is made by brewing civet coffee first, then mixed with durian flesh and stirred until evenly distributed.
